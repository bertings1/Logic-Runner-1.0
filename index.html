Quick Tips
  1. Drag operator names to symbols, or answer prompts when shown.

  3. Use Retry / Quit from the floating controls while playing.
Tutorial

How to Play

  1. Choose difficulty and mode, then press Start.
  2. Questions are randomized and unique per difficulty (no repeats across difficulties).
  3. In Timed mode the floating timer starts when the first question appears.
  4. Use the floating Retry or Quit buttons during a run.
