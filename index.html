Quick Tips
  1. Drag operator names to symbols or fill the correct answer!
  2. Timed mode gives a time limit for each question.
  3. Use Retry or Quit to restart or exit anytime.
  4. Score points for correct logic and table fills!

How to Play

  1. Choose your difficulty and mode
  2. For each question, follow the prompt and drag/fill/select correct logic
  3. Timed mode = more challenge, higher score
