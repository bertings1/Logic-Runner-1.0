Connectors & Semantics

AND (∧) — true only when both p and q are true.
OR (∨) — true when at least one of p or q is true.
NOT (¬) — inverts truth.
IMPLIES (→) — false only when p is true and q is false.

About

Logic Runner helps students practice propositional logic under mild time pressure and understand truth tables, operators, and reasoning.
